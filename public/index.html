Fabric Care

General Care: Machine wash cold. Do not bleach. Tumble dry low. Iron on low heat.

Cotton: Wash inside out, avoid harsh detergents, air dry for longevity.

Linen: Gentle cycle, remove promptly, iron while damp.

Wool/Blends: Dry clean recommended or hand wash cold, lay flat to dry.

About COLONIAL

Founded in 2026, COLONIAL is a contemporary fashion brand that blends timeless elegance with modern minimalist design. We believe in quality craftsmanship, sustainable practices, and creating pieces that transcend seasons.

Our collections are inspired by the raw beauty of nature and the precision of architecture. Every garment is designed with care, using premium materials sourced responsibly.

We are proud to serve customers across Bangladesh and look forward to bringing our vision worldwide.
